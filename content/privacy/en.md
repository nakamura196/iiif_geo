# Privacy Policy

This page explains how IIIF Georeference Viewer ("this site") handles visitor information.

## Analytics (Google Analytics)

To understand usage and improve the service, this site uses Google Analytics 4, an analytics tool provided by Google LLC.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as the following. This information does not identify you personally.

- Date and time of access, pages viewed, and time spent
- Referrer (the URL or search keywords you came from)
- IP address (anonymized by default in Google Analytics 4)
- Browser, OS, and device type, screen size, and approximate region

The collected information is transmitted to and stored on Google's servers and is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policy. For details, please refer to:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites?hl=en)

## Opting out

If you wish to stop data collection by Google Analytics, you can disable cookies in your browser or install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en).

## Transmission to third parties

As described above, this site transmits a portion of your browsing information to Google (a third party) for analytics. The information transmitted, the recipient, and the purpose are as stated on this page.

## Changes to this policy

This site may revise this policy as needed. Any revisions take effect once posted on this page.
